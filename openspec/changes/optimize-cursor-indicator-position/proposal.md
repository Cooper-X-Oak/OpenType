# Change Proposal: optimize-cursor-indicator-position

## Why
<!-- What problem are we solving? Why is this change necessary? -->
用户反馈当前的光标跟随指示器位置距离光标稍远，需要优化其位置，使其更加紧凑和符合预期。

## What Changes
<!-- What will we build or modify? -->
调整 `CursorIndicator` 的位置偏移量，使其更靠近鼠标光标。

## Impact
<!-- Performance, security, or compatibility implications -->
*   **交互体验**: 提升视觉反馈的紧凑感。
